About Sigmamc

SigmaMC is a server created by 0mnikron in order to bring players a fun and unique survival Minecraft experiences. We will constantly be adding new plugins that enhance the vanilla survival Minecraft experience.

The plugins are made by Microbox_ and include the runes plugin and many more in the future!

So why not hop on! It's free!
